About Us

We are a group of Italian youngsters hailing from Cuneo, united by the same passion: technology. We embarked on a mission to offer individuals affected by ALS the opportunity to begin a rehabilitation journey, having fun and engaging with cutting-edge tools such as the Hololens 2, an augmented reality headset. We believe we have successfully achieved our goal and are eager to take on new challenges. We are the "HOLOTRAINERS".

Our Team

The Team

Simone Arrigoni

Group leader and manager, he had the most impact on the project because he tried his hardest to get to know as much as possible about Microsoft Hololens 2 and, thanks to Leonardo Pollicino, he managed to transfer the Unity projects into Hololens 2

Karol Bruno

His role in the team was converting all of the ideas into code, assigning the scripts to the GameObjects, and doing research on the coding language C#. Leonardo Pollicino

Leonardo Pollicino had the most impact regarding the implementation of Unity in Hololens 2, he has also looked into the C# programming language to help Karol Bruno and Marco Masoero scripting the GameObjects.
